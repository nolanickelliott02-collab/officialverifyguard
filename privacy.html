Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 8, 2026

VerifyGuard ("we," "our," "us") builds tools to help you identify scams, deepfakes, and misinformation. This Privacy Policy explains what information VerifyGuard collects, how it's used, and the choices you have. It applies to the VerifyGuard iOS app and the officialverifyguard.com website.

1. Information we collect

Account information. If you create an account, we collect your email address and a securely hashed password, or, if you use Sign in with Apple, the identifier and email Apple provides. You can also use VerifyGuard as a guest without an account.

Content you submit for scanning. When you use a scan feature, we process only what you actively submit:

  • Photos you choose from your library or camera, for AI-generated / deepfake and manipulation detection.
  • Links or text you enter or scan from Control Center (via clipboard access), for scam and phishing detection.
  • News search terms you enter, to check outlet coverage and bias.

VerifyGuard does not scan your photo library, clipboard, or browsing activity in the background. Scans only happen when you initiate them.

Usage data. We keep a daily count of scans used against your plan's limit, and basic app diagnostics (crash logs, feature usage) to keep the service reliable.

Purchase information. Subscription purchases are handled entirely by Apple's App Store / StoreKit. We receive confirmation of your active tier, not your payment details.

2. How we use your information

  • To generate a scan result (scam risk, deepfake likelihood, news bias) for content you submit.
  • To enforce daily scan limits tied to your subscription tier.
  • To send scan results and, on Pro and Guardian plans, real-time scam alert notifications.
  • To operate the AI pattern engine that surfaces emerging scam trends in the app's Scampedia feed. This uses aggregated pattern data, not your personal content.
  • To provide customer support when you contact us.
  • To maintain security and prevent abuse of the service.

3. Third-party services we use

To produce accurate scan results, specific content is shared with the following providers, solely to generate your result:

5. Data retention

Scan history is stored on your device and, if you're signed in, associated with your account so it syncs across sessions. You can delete your scan history in-app. Account data is retained until you delete your account; you can request deletion at any time by contacting us below.

6. Your choices

  • Use VerifyGuard as a guest to avoid creating an account.
  • Disable notifications at any time in iOS Settings.
  • Request access to, correction of, or deletion of your data by emailing us.
  • Manage or cancel your subscription anytime via the App Store.

7. Children's privacy

VerifyGuard is not directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, contact us and we will delete it.

8. Security

We use industry-standard measures, including password hashing and encrypted network connections, to protect your information. No method of transmission or storage is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

9. Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Material changes will be reflected by an updated "Last updated" date above, and significant changes may be communicated in-app.

10. Contact us

Questions about this policy or your data? Email supportverifyguard@gmail.com.
