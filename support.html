FAQ

Frequently asked questions

How do photo and video scans work?

VerifyGuard runs on-device Vision and language analysis on every scan, then — depending on your plan — sends the image to our AI detection provider for deepfake and manipulation analysis. Links are checked against domain reputation data and 70+ antivirus engines. We only send the minimum data needed to produce a result; see our Privacy Policy for details.

Why was my scan flagged as a scam or fake, and I disagree?

VerifyGuard's verdicts are automated risk assessments, not a guarantee. If you believe a result is wrong, email us with the item you scanned (a screenshot or the link) and we'll take a look and use it to improve detection.

How do I upgrade, downgrade, or cancel my subscription?

All subscriptions are billed and managed through the App Store. On your iPhone, go to Settings → your name → Subscriptions → VerifyGuard to change or cancel your plan. Changes take effect at the end of the current billing period. Full terms are in our Terms of Use.

I have a promo code — where do I enter it?

Open VerifyGuard → Settings → Promo Code, and enter your code there. Valid codes unlock the matching plan tier for the stated duration.

What permissions does VerifyGuard need, and why?

Photos (to scan an image you choose), Clipboard (to scan a link or text from the Control Center widget), Notifications (for scan results and, on Pro/Guardian, real-time scam alerts), and Network (to reach our scanning services). VerifyGuard never scans your photo library in the background — only images you actively select.

Is my data shared with anyone?

Only the specific content you submit for scanning is sent to our detection providers, and only to produce your result. We do not sell your data. Full details are in our Privacy Policy.

The app isn't working / a scan is stuck — what do I do?

First, check your internet connection and try again. If it persists, email us with your device model, iOS version, and what you were trying to scan — that's usually enough for us to diagnose it quickly.
